creamy chorizo pasta

serves 2

ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large pinch of chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 chorizo ring, sliced and quartered
  • 2 medium-sized chicken breasts, diced / 150g quorn chicken pieces
  • 2 or 3 cloves of garlic, grated
  • 100ml double cream or crème fraîche
  • 100g spinach
  • 100g cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 150g pasta, your choice
  • a splash of olive oil

instructions

  1. Prepare your garlic, cherry tomatoes, chorizo and (if using) chicken, as directed in the ingredients.
  2. In a frying pan, heat the splash of olive oil on a medium-low heat, then add the grated garlic. Sauté the garlic until it is on the cusp of browning (this won't take long).
  3. Add in your quartered chorizo and gently fry for 3-5 minutes until its rich red colour starts to run.
  4. Add in the quartered cherry tomatoes and sauté until they start to wilt. By this point, a sauce should be starting to form with the juice from the chorizo and tomatoes. Meanwhile, bring a second pot of salted water to the boil and add the pasta. Return to a light simmer and cook as instructed on the packet, or to-taste.
  5. As your pasta is cooking, put the spinach in a colander and place on top of the pasta pot. Steam the spinach until all of it has started to wilt (this won't take long). Leave to one side.
  6. Add in your diced chicken breast or quorn chicken pieces to the frying pan. Cook for 3-5 minutes until the quorn chicken pieces are starting to brown, or the chicken breast is half-cooked. At which point, add in the crème fraîche or double cream, some water, and the seasoning.
  7. Mix together to create a creamy sauce, and allow to simmer until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Drain the steamed spinach and squeeze out any excess liquid - be careful not to burn yourself here. Add in the steamed spinach to the pan and mix through.
  8. Drain the pasta and add into the frying pan. Mix through and taste - adjust seasoning if necessary.
  9. If desired, serve up and grate some parmesan over the dish. Otherwise, voilà!
